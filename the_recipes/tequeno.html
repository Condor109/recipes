TEQUEÑOS

DESCRIPTION

Tequeños is usually described as a fried breaded cheese stick or spear of bread dough with fresh cheese stuffed in the middle. They are eaten as snacks in parties or as a side appetizer. Their name comes from Los Teques, a city in Venezuela where a wealthy family invented them.

There are multiple variations, such as Peruvian Tequeños, where Wantán dough is used and other fillings can be used. In this recipe we will follow the previously mentioned dish, as in my opinion it is more simple. And no, there will be no recipe for guacamole. I mean, you just have to destroy your avocado and add lemon onion and tomato.

INGREDIENTS

  • Wantan dough (buy or made it)
  • Cheese sticks
  • Oil
  • Water

STEPS

  1. Cover the borders of the slices of dough with water
  2. Add the cheese stick in the center
  3. Close the dough
  4. Add oil in the pan
  5. Fry all the tequeños

That's all, enjoy it
