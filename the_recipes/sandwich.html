MIX SANDWICH

DESCRIPTION

According to Wikipedia, the sandwich is named after John Montagu, 4th Earl of Sandwich, an eighteenth-century English aristocrat.[8][9] It is commonly said that Lord Sandwich, during long sessions of cribbage and other card games at public gambling houses, would order his valet to bring him salt beef between two pieces of toasted bread.

That's why we are going to make a mix sandwich. We need to honor this man, although I don't have an idea if it was a good person or no. I mean, we need an excuse to fill this paragraph, don't we? Anyway,let's stop saying bullshit. This recipe will teach you to prepare a complicated mix sandwich with only 3 ingredients and 4 steps.Are you ready?

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Egg
  • Oil

STEPS

  1. Put a slice of bread
  2. Put the cheese
  3. Put the ham
  4. Close with the other slice of bread
  5. Put the sandwich in a microwave or oven until cheese melts
  6. Fry the egg
  7. Take out the sandwich
  8. Put the egg inside or over it

That's all, enjoy it
