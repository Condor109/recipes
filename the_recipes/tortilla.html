FLOUR TORTILLA

DESCRIPTION

There is not much to say, as its name says all what we need to know. Basically, is a flattened bread without yeast, very popular in the United States of Mexico, where are used to cook delicious quesadillas, altholught it is also prepared in the United States of America and Centroamerican countries.

The fluor tortilla is used in different preparations, such as burritos, gorditas, tacos, and the previously mentioned quesadillas. However, as it's usage cover many countries, it can be found with sugar, butter, jam and even Nutella. Also it has different preparations, but in this case we will use the most common one, I think.

INGREDIENTS

  • FLOUR
  • WATER
  • SALT
  • BUTTER

STEPS

  1. Put flour on a pot
  2. Add a bit of butter
  3. Add a bit of a spoon of salt
  4. Add enough water
  5. Knead until a dough is formed
  6. If it's too clingly add more flour
  7. Divide the dough in little portions if it's too big
  8. Put it in a pan and cook

That's all, enjoy
